Ljubljana, Slovenia: Map

Ljubljana, Slovenia Dragon
We had quite a bit of time in Ljubljana to discover on our own. I was fascinated by this alley way art installation. I tried to get an image that captures it all, but ... a brain on a hand on a pedastal... I couldn't fit it all in.
Lake Bled has a rich tradition of boats and the men who row them are as skilled as Venitian Gondoliers.
The Church on the Island at Lake Bled has a bell and a legend. If you can make the bell ring 3 times, your wish will come true. It's surprisingly more complicated than it sounds, hence the victory stance.
